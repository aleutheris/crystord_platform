Crystord Google Add-on

A streamlined Google Workspace Add-on that brings the power of Crystord's synchronization directly to your Google Sheets™ and Google Slides™. Transform static presentations into dynamic, data-driven templates with just a few clicks.

How It Works

Dynamic Content Synchronization

The Crystord Google Add-on transforms your Google Slides™ into dynamic, data-driven presentations. Simply mark variables in your slides using curly brackets (e.g., {{ '{' }}{{ '{' }}ItemName{{ '}' }}{{ '}' }}) and sync them with values from your Google Sheets™ at the click of a button.

  • Copy your Sheets™ file ID
  • Paste it into the add-on
  • Click sync to update instantly

Setting up variables in your Google Slides™ presentation
