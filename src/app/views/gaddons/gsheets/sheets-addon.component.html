Google Sheets™ Add-on

Connect Crystord's powerful data flow engine directly to Google Sheets. Fetch, visualise, and collaborate with granular versioned data—all within your familiar spreadsheet environment.

Key Features

Selective Data Fetching

Pull only the data you need from Crystord. No unnecessary overhead—just the atoms and flows relevant to your analysis.

Granular Versioning

Access historical versions of any data point. Track how values evolved over time, right in your spreadsheet.

Team Collaboration

Store data logic in Crystord whilst visualising in Sheets. Team members see live updates with a single source of truth.

Custom Formatting

Define your own data formats and transformations. Crystord delivers data exactly how you need it.

How It Works

1
Install the Add-on

Download the Crystord for Google Sheets add-on from the Google Workspace Marketplace. It's free to install and works with any Crystord account.

2
Connect Your Account

Authorise the add-on to access your Crystord workspace. Your credentials are securely stored and never shared.

3
Query Your Data

Use the add-on sidebar to browse your Crystord data flows. Select the atoms and nodes you want to import into your sheet.

4
Visualise & Analyse

Data appears in your sheet, formatted as you specified. Use Google Sheets' native tools for charts, pivot tables, and more.
