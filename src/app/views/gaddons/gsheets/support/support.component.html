Introduction

Welcome to the Crystord Google Sheets™ Add-on support page. Here you'll find resources to help you get started, answers to common questions, troubleshooting steps, and ways to contact us.

Getting Started

What is Crystord?

Crystord is a data-flow platform that stores versioned "atoms" and their attributes on secure Crystord-managed servers. The Crystord Google Sheets™ add-on lets you search and couple spreadsheet cells to those atoms directly from a sidebar, ensuring your sheet always reflects the freshest data coming from Crystord.

Basic setup:

  • Install the Crystord Google Sheets™ Add-on from the Google Workspace Marketplace.
  • Open any Google Sheets™ document and go to Extensions → Crystord → Authorise to connect your Crystord account.
  • Click the Crystord icon in the sidebar to open the add-on interface.
  • The sidebar will allow you to:
    • Browse Data: Search and explore your Crystord atoms and data flows.
    • Select Cells: Choose where to insert data in your spreadsheet.
    • Fetch Data: Import atom values with granular versioning support.
    • Refresh: Update existing data with the latest values from Crystord.
  • The first step is to authorise your Crystord account, as nothing works without it. After authorisation, you can browse your data flows and import atoms into your spreadsheet cells.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install the add-on?

Install directly via the Google Workspace Marketplace or from within Google Sheets™ by searching for "Crystord - Control Your Dataflow".

How do I connect my Crystord account?

After installation, go to Extensions → Crystord → Authorise in any Google Sheets document. Sign in with your Crystord credentials. You may use your Google account for convenience.

The data isn't updating in my sheet.

  • Ensure you have authorised your Crystord account and have permissions to access the data.
  • Check that you're selecting valid cells for data insertion.
  • Click the "Refresh" button in the sidebar to update with the latest values from Crystord.

What data types are supported?

The add-on supports text and numeric values from Crystord atoms. Complex data structures are automatically formatted for spreadsheet compatibility.

What if I accidentally delete imported data?

You can always re-import data from Crystord using the sidebar. The original data remains safely stored in your Crystord workspace.

Can I use the add-on offline?

No, the add-on requires an internet connection to fetch data from Crystord servers. However, once data is imported into your sheet, you can work with it offline using Google Sheets' offline mode.

Troubleshooting

Add-on not appearing?

Refresh Google Sheets™ and ensure you are using the same account you installed the add-on with.

Sidebar won't open or is unresponsive?

Try closing and reopening Google Sheets™, or reinstall the add-on if needed.

Authentication failed?

Your Crystord session may have expired. Go to Extensions → Crystord → Authorise to log in again with your correct credentials.

Data loading slowly or not at all?

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify you have permissions to access the Crystord data
  • Try fetching a smaller dataset first
  • Check the browser console (F12) for error messages

Is the Add-on updated?

Ensure you are using the latest version of the Crystord Add-on. Update it via the Google Workspace Marketplace if needed.
