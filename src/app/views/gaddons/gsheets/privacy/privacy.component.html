Crystord Google Sheets™ Add-on: Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 14, 2025

Introduction

Crystord is a data-flow platform that stores versioned "atoms" and their attributes on secure Crystord managed servers. The Crystord Google Sheets™ add-on lets you search and couple spreadsheet cells to those atoms directly from a sidebar, ensuring your sheet always reflects the freshest data coming from Crystord. This privacy notice explains how information is accessed, stored, and protected when you sign in with your Google account and use the add-on.

Permissions and Data Access

When you authorise the add-on, Google prompts you to grant the following scopes so the sidebar can function:

  • Read the active Google Sheets™ file to determine which cells you selected, and also to read the metadata of those cells stored in a hidden spreadsheet.
  • Write or update those cells when you refresh or sync atom data.
  • Identify you via your Google account so we can link the add-on session to your Crystord workspace.

These permissions are used exclusively to let you fetch atom features, search by labels, and push refreshed values into the currently opened sheet.

Use of Google User Data

Crystord's use of Google User Data follows Google's Limited Use Requirements:

  • Google account information is used only to authenticate you and show data that belongs to your Crystord workspace.
  • Sheet content is accessed only when you initiate a cell-atom coupling or refresh action.
  • We never use Google data for advertising, profiling, or resale and we do not share it with unaffiliated third parties.

Data Storage

  • Atom Repository: Data you manage inside Crystord, including atom labels, atom bonds, atom feature values, is stored on Crystord servers so that it can be versioned, searched, and fetched by the add-on.
  • Cell-to-atom Mappings: When you couple a cell to an atom feature, the mapping (spreadsheet ID, sheet name, cell reference, atom identifier, and the feature name) is stored in your sheet metadata. These mappings are retained while active but can be removed by decoupling cells.
  • No Bulk Sheet Storage: The add-on does not upload your entire Google Sheet to Crystord. Only the pairings you create and the resulting atom data are retained.

Data Processing and Security

All Google Sheet operations happen through Google APIs while Crystord handles atom lookups, label searches, and version history on its servers. Communications between the sidebar, Google, and Crystord use HTTPS with modern encryption. Access to Crystord infrastructure is audited and limited to authorised personnel following least-privilege principles.

Exception Logging

Crystord keeps technical error logs (without sheet content) for up to 30 days to diagnose issues such as failed refreshes or sidebar crashes.

User Controls

  • You can uncouple any cell inside the sidebar, which immediately removes the mapping from Crystord storage.
  • You can revoke Google permissions at any time through Google Account settings, which stops the add-on from reading or writing sheets.
  • You can delete atoms from within Crystord, which removes the underlying server-side data the add-on references.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention

  • Atom Data: Atoms persist in the Crystord database even when decoupled from cells, until explicitly deleted by the user.
  • Cell-to-atom Mappings: Mappings are retained while active in your sheet metadata to enable refresh operations.
  • Google Data: Sheet values are only read transiently to insert refreshed atom data. They are not stored outside Google.
  • Exception Logs: Technical error logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging purposes.

Data Deletion

  • Atom Deletion: Users can delete atoms from their Crystord workspace, which removes the underlying server-side data.
  • Mapping Removal: Decoupling cells removes the mapping but preserves the atom data until user deletion.
  • Access Revocation: Revoking permissions terminates all data access and stops synchronization.
  • User Rights: You have the right to request confirmation of data deletion by contacting us at aleutheris@gmail.com.

Data Minimization

Crystord follows data minimization principles by:

  • Only accessing the specific Google Sheets™ files you explicitly authorize.
  • Processing data only when you initiate actions like pairing or refreshing.
  • Not collecting or storing personal information beyond what's necessary for the add-on's functionality.
  • Automatically purging any temporary data after each session.

Compliance

Crystord's use and transfer of information obtained from Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We also follow applicable data protection laws in the regions where we operate.
