Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 14, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Crystord ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Crystord for Google Sheets™ add-on (the "Add-on").

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you connect your Crystord account to the Add-on, we collect your Crystord username and authentication credentials.
  • Spreadsheet Data: We access only the specific data you request to import from Crystord into your Google Sheets.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the Add-on, including features used and actions performed.
  • Technical Information: We may collect device information, browser type, and IP addresses for security and troubleshooting purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Add-on functionality
  • Authenticate your Crystord account and authorise data access
  • Fetch and synchronise data between Crystord and Google Sheets
  • Improve and optimise the Add-on's performance
  • Provide customer support and respond to your enquiries
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and security threats

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: We may share information when you explicitly authorise us to do so.
  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist in operating the Add-on.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Google API Services User Data Policy

The Crystord for Google Sheets Add-on's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the Add-on services and fulfil the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Objection to or restriction of data processing
  • Data portability

Children's Privacy

The Add-on is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
