Crystord: Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

Introduction

Crystord is a software platform that enables synchronisation and management of data between various sources. The Crystord Google Workspace Add-on provides features similar to the core Crystord platform, allowing users to seamlessly integrate and update content between Google Sheets™ and Google Slides™. This privacy policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Crystord platform and its Google Add-on.

Data Collection and Access

When you use the Crystord platform or install the Crystord Google Add-on, the application accesses the following specific Google user data:

  • Google Sheets™ Content: Read access to the specific spreadsheets you select to synchronize data.
  • Google Slides™ Content: Read and write access to the specific presentations you are editing to update charts or text.

These permissions are required solely to enable the synchronisation and management features provided by Crystord.

Data Usage

Crystord's use of your Google User Data accessed from Google APIs adheres to Google's Limited Use Requirements. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Synchronize Content: Transfer data values from your Sheets to your Slides presentations upon your request.
  • Display Information: Show your spreadsheet data within the add-on sidebar to help you select what to sync.

Crystord does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any unauthorized purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not share your Google User Data with any third parties. Specifically:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your data is not sold, traded, or transferred to outside parties.
  • No AI Training: Your data is not used to train artificial intelligence models.

Data is only transferred to Google's servers as necessary to execute the add-on's functions (e.g., calling the Slides API).

Data Storage and Protection

  • No External Storage: Crystord does not store your Google Sheets™ or Slides™ data on external servers. All data resides within your own Google Drive.
  • Security Measures: All communication between the add-on and Google services occurs over HTTPS. Since the application runs within Google Apps Script, it benefits from Google's native security infrastructure.

Exception Logging

Crystord uses Google's Cloud Logging for exception logging. This logging is limited to technical error messages and does not include user content or personal data. Exception logs are used solely for debugging and reliability improvements.

User Controls

  • You can revoke the add-on's access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account security settings (https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
  • You may uninstall the add-on or discontinue use of the Crystord platform whenever you wish.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention

  • Google User Data: Crystord does not retain your Google Sheets™ or Google Slides™ content on external servers. All data operations occur within Google's infrastructure and your Google account.
  • Configuration Data: Any configuration settings (such as Sheet™ IDs or names) are stored within your Slides™ data properties and are not transmitted to external servers.
  • Exception Logs: Technical error logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging purposes, then automatically deleted.

Data Deletion

  • Automatic Deletion: Since Crystord operates entirely within Google's environment, no user data is stored externally that requires deletion.
  • Access Revocation: When you revoke the add-on's permissions through your Google Account settings, all access to your data is immediately terminated.
  • Add-on Removal: Uninstalling the add-on removes all associated permissions and stops any data access.
  • User Rights: You have the right to request confirmation that no personal data is being stored externally by contacting us at aleutheris@gmail.com.

Compliance

Crystord's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use Requirements. This means your data is used only as necessary to provide or improve user-facing features and is never sold or used for unauthorized purposes.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the Crystord platform, please contact the developer at aleutheris@gmail.com.
