Crystord: Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 30, 2025

Introduction

Crystord is a software platform that enables synchronization and management of data between various sources. The Crystord Google Workspace Add-on provides features similar to the core Crystord platform, allowing users to seamlessly integrate and update content between Google Sheets™ and Google Slides™. This privacy policy explains how your information is handled when you use the Crystord platform and its Google Add-on.

Permissions and Data Access

When you use the Crystord platform or install the Crystord Google Add-on, you are prompted to grant the following permissions:

  • Read data from your Google Sheets™
  • Update your current Google Slides™ presentation

These permissions are required solely to enable the synchronization and management features provided by Crystord.

Use of Google User Data

Crystord's use of your Google User Data accessed from Google APIs adheres to Google's Limited Use Requirements. Specifically:

  • Crystord accesses your Google User Data exclusively through Google-provided APIs.
  • Data is used only to deliver user-facing features that are prominent in the Crystord platform and add-on interfaces.
  • Crystord does not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any unauthorized purposes.

Data Storage

  • No External Storage: Crystord does not store your Google Sheets™ or Slides™ data on external servers.
  • No Data Sharing: Your Google User Data is not shared with third parties, including the developer's own servers, except as required to provide the core features or as permitted by Google's policies. All data remains within Google's secure infrastructure unless otherwise disclosed.

Data Processing and Security

All data processing related to Google Sheets and Slides occurs within your Google account and the Google Apps Script environment. Crystord operates entirely within the secure environment provided by Google Workspace, following Google's security and privacy controls.

Exception Logging

Crystord uses Google's Cloud Logging for exception logging. This logging is limited to technical error messages and does not include user content or personal data. Exception logs are used solely for debugging and reliability improvements.

User Controls

  • You can revoke the add-on's access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account security settings.
  • You may uninstall the add-on or discontinue use of the Crystord platform whenever you wish.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data Retention

  • Google User Data: Crystord does not retain your Google Sheets™ or Google Slides™ content on external servers. All data operations occur within Google's infrastructure and your Google account.
  • Configuration Data: Any configuration settings (such as Sheet™ IDs or names) are stored within your Slides™ data properties and are not transmitted to external servers.
  • Exception Logs: Technical error logs are retained for up to 30 days for debugging purposes, then automatically deleted.

Data Deletion

  • Automatic Deletion: Since Crystord operates entirely within Google's environment, no user data is stored externally that requires deletion.
  • Access Revocation: When you revoke the add-on's permissions through your Google Account settings, all access to your data is immediately terminated.
  • Add-on Removal: Uninstalling the add-on removes all associated permissions and stops any data access.
  • User Rights: You have the right to request confirmation that no personal data is being stored externally by contacting us at aleutheris@gmail.com.

Data Minimization

Crystord follows data minimization principles by:

  • Only accessing the specific Google Sheets™ and Google Slides™ files you explicitly authorize.
  • Processing data only within Google's secure environment.
  • Not collecting or storing personal information beyond what's necessary for the add-on's functionality.
  • Automatically purging any temporary data after each session.

Compliance

Crystord's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use Requirements. This means your data is used only as necessary to provide or improve user-facing features and is never sold or used for unauthorized purposes.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or the Crystord platform, please contact the developer at aleutheris@gmail.com.
