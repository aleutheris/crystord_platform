How It Works

Dynamic Content Synchronization

The Crystord Google Workspace Add-on transforms your Google Slides™ into dynamic, data-driven presentations. Simply mark variables in your slides using curly brackets (e.g., {{ '{' }}{{ '{' }}item_name{{ '}' }}{{ '}' }}) and sync them with values from your Google Sheets™ at the click of a button.

  • Open Google Slides™ and click the expansion arrow to find Crystord in the right sidebar
  • Create your database, open it and add your variables
  • Click sync to update your slides immediately
